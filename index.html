What indicator did I choose?

Premium to NAV

Premium to NAV measures how far Strategy's equity value trades above or below the spot value of the bitcoin sitting on its balance sheet.

Why choose it?

It captures valuation heat

Unlike raw price alone, the premium isolates whether investors are paying extra for treasury strategy, access, leverage, execution, or optionality beyond the underlying BTC.

Relationship with BTC

It shows whether equity is outrunning the asset

When Bitcoin rises and the premium expands, investors are paying even more for the corporate wrapper. When Bitcoin rises but the premium compresses, the stock is lagging the treasury itself.

DAT.co lens

Built around treasury-company thinking

A better DAT.co product should not only show whether the stock is expensive. It should also surface the treasury per share, dilution path, and how the capital machine is compounding BTC.

Formula

Premium to NAV

((share price × common shares outstanding)
÷ (bitcoin held × BTC price)) − 1

A value above zero means Strategy trades above the spot value of its bitcoin. A value below zero means the stock sits below treasury value.
